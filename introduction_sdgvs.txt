SDGVS stands for Smart Digital Gram Vikas System.
It is a modern mobile application for village communities.
The app connects villagers to the Gram Panchayat digitally.
It provides a digital platform for all official announcements.
It features a simple design that anyone can understand.
The app makes communication faster and more transparent.
SDGVS is a complete digital solution for village management.
It helps every villager stay updated and connected.
It brings transparency to the village administration.
The system is easy to use for people of all ages.
It helps build a smart and digitally empowered village.

Purpose of SDGVS:
1. The main purpose is to modernize village administration.
2. It aims to make government services reachable for everyone.
3. The app ensures that every citizen gets timely information.
4. It connects villagers directly with the Gram Panchayat.
5. It empowers villagers with knowledge and technology.
6. SDGVS aims to build a better future for every village.
7. It provides a digital record of all village activities.
8. It reduces paper work and makes the process faster.
9. It ensures all government schemes reach the villagers.
10. It allows villagers to file digital complaints easily.

Scope of SDGVS:
SDGVS is used for villages under the Gram Panchayat.
It provides digital notices, government schemes, and village news for villagers.
It keeps records of village data and assets in one place.
It helps manage certificates and track village achievements.
It connects villagers, Gram Panchayat, and village leaders through one platform.
It supports offline data syncing for areas with poor or no internet.
SDGVS helps in the development of a smart and well-managed village.
It provides a way for villagers to give feedback on local projects.
It helps in tracking village progress and growth over time.
It makes the entire village management system transparent and fair.
It ensures that every citizen gets timely information.

Limitation of SDGVS:
1. The app needs a smartphone which some villagers may not have.
2. It requires internet for full features, which is not always available.
4. The app depends on correct data entered by the Panchayat admin.
5. Real-time updates are only possible when the device is online.
6. The app does not support very old Android or iOS versions.
8. It may take time for all villagers to trust and adopt the system.

Technologies used in SDGVS:
| Category | Technology / Tools |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Frontend** | **Flutter (Dart)** for Citizen App with **Hive** for local storage; **React.js (JavaScript)** for Admin and Super Admin Dashboard. |
| **Backend** | **Node.js** with **Express.js** implementing a REST API. |
| **Database** | **PostgreSQL** for relational data management and UUID-based primary keys. |
| **IDE** | **Visual Studio Code** and **Android Studio** for cross-platform development. |
| **Tools for Diagram** | **draw.io** for System Architecture and Database ERD modeling. |

Functional Requirements of SDGVS:
(Based on actual app features)

A. Onboarding and Authentication
1. The app shall show an onboarding screen to new users with app information.
2. The system shall allow users to register using their mobile number and OTP.
3. Users shall set up their profile with name, photo, gender, DOB, and address.
4. The system shall support login for both Gramin users and Panchayat admins.
5. The system shall redirect users to the correct dashboard based on their role.

B. Home and Dashboard
1. The app shall show a home screen with quick access to all main features.
2. The dashboard shall display village news, notices, and important updates.

C. Notice Board
1. The system shall display official notices posted by the Gram Panchayat.
2. Users shall be able to read the full detail of each notice.

D. Complaint Management
1. Users shall be able to choose a complaint category before filing.
2. Users shall Submit a complaint with a description, photo, or voice note.
3. Users shall be able to view the current status of all their complaints.
4. The system shall show a success screen after a complaint is submitted.

E. Government Schemes
1. The system shall show a list of available government schemes.
2. Users shall be able to view full details and eligibility of each scheme.

F. Agriculture and Farmer Advisory
1. The system shall show real-time Mandi market prices for farm products.
2. The system shall show weather alerts relevant to farming.
3. The system shall include a crop calendar with planting and harvest guidance.

G. Document Locker
1. The system shall allow users to store digital copies of their documents.

H. Village News Feed
1. The system shall display a local news feed for village-related good news.
2. Users shall be able to read full news articles on a detail screen.

I. Community Polls
1. Admins shall be able to create polls for village development decisions.
2. Villagers shall be able to vote on active polls and see results.

J. Impact Dashboard
1. The app shall show village-level impact statistics and progress.
2. Users shall be able to see how the Panchayat is growing over time.

K. Rewards and Recognition
1. The system shall show a leaderboard of top contributing citizens.
2. The system shall generate certificates for users based on achievements.

L. Chatbot Support
1. The app shall include an AI chatbot to answer user queries.

M. Emergency Services
1. The app shall provide a list of emergency contacts (Police, Ambulance, Fire).
2. Users shall be able to call emergency services directly from the app.

N. Feedback
1. Users shall be able to submit feedback about village services.

O. Notifications
1. The system shall send notifications for new notices and important alerts.

P. Settings and Profile
1. Users shall be able to edit their profile including photo, name, and details.
2. Users shall be able to manage their notification and account settings.

Non-Functional Requirements of SDGVS:

A. User Friendliness
The application should use simple icons and visuals so that villagers can easily understand it.
The design of the app should be clean and easy to navigate.
The system should support voice reading of notices for users who have difficulty reading.

B. System Speed
The application should load quickly even on slow internet connections like 3G or 4G.
Important data such as notices should be stored locally so users can view them offline.
The system should allow many users to use the app at the same time without slowing down.

C. Data Protection
All personal data should be safely encrypted while storing and sending through the internet.
User login sessions should be stored safely on the device.

D. System Stability
The application should work properly even in areas with weak internet connectivity.
Data should automatically synchronize with the server when the internet connection becomes available.
