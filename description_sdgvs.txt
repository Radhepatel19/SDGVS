# SDGVS: Smart Digital Gramin Vikas System
## Comprehensive Application Overview

### 1. Introduction & Purpose
The **Smart Digital Gramin Vikas System (SDGVS)** is a transformative digital ecosystem designed to catalyze rural development in India. it serves as a centralized bridge between **Gramin Users (Villagers)** and **Gram Panchayat Administration**, replacing slow, paper-based processes with real-time, transparent digital solutions.

**Primary Goals:**
- **Absolute Transparency**: Real-time tracking of grievances and community projects.
- **Information Democratization**: Instant access to government schemes and village notices.
- **Digital Security**: Secure storage for sensitive identity documents.
- **Economic Empowerment**: Data-driven agricultural support for farmers.

---

### 2. Core Modules & Features

#### A. Governance & Complaint Redressal
- **Multimedia Filing**: Users can register complaints using text, camera photos, or recorded voice notes.
- **Lifecycle Tracking**: Real-time status updates (Pending → In Progress → Resolved) with direct admin feedback/remarks.
- **Feedback Loop**: Users can rate the resolution quality once a complaint is closed.

#### B. Agri-Tech Suite (Farmer Advisory)
- **Mandi Price Hub**: Real-time market rates for regional crops with trend indicators (up/down).
- **Weather Alerts**: Localized weather forecasts with specific warnings for agricultural planning.
- **Crop Calendar**: Seasonal guidance for sowing, irrigation, and harvesting cycles tailored to the local climate.

#### C. Information Hub (Notice Board & Schemes)
- **Digital Notice Board**: High-priority announcements (Water, Power, General) with Text-to-Speech (TTS) support for accessibility.
- **Scheme Directory**: Categorized directory (Education, Farming, Health) of welfare schemes with eligibility checklists and application guides.

#### D. Security & Digital Locker
- **Identity Vault**: A secure "Personal Cloud" for storing Aadhaar, PAN, and Ration Cards.

---

### 3. Gamification & Community (New Features)

#### E. Impact Dashboard
- **Community Impact Score**: A personalized score (e.g., 850) that grows as users file complaints or apply for schemes.
- **Stats Overview**: Tracks "Complaints Resolved," "Schemes Applied," and "Contribution Hours."
- **Badges**: Earnable digital tokens like *Active Citizen*, *Social Helper*, and *Eco-Warrior*.

#### F. Good News Feed
- **Positive Journalism**: A dedicated feed for village success stories, solar installations, or scholarship winners.
- **Engagement**: Users can "Like" stories and share their own village developments to foster community pride.

#### G. Rewards & Leaderboard
- **Village Heroes**: Monthly rankings across three categories: Citizens, Students, and Farmers.
- **Podium View**: Visual highlights for top-3 performers.
- **Digital Certificates**: Automatically generated "Certificates of Excellence" for monthly winners, which can be downloaded or shared.

---

### 4. Technical Architecture & Database

The system is built on a **PostgreSQL** relational backend, utilizing **UUIDs** for high-performance indexing and data integrity.

**Key Database Tables (Updated):**
- **`users`**: Stores profile data including email, gender, dob, address, and occupation.
- **`complaints`**: Tracks status, admin remarks, and multimedia URLs.
- **`good_news`**: Manages the community feed with likes and category-based icons.
- **`user_impact_stats`**: Dynamically calculates scores based on user activity.
- **`reward_winners`**: A historical record of monthly achievements and certificates.
- **`user_badges`**: Links achievement icons to active users.

---

### 5. Detailed Activity Flows

1. **Onboarding**: Mobile/OTP Auth → Profile Setup (Name, Village, Occupation).
2. **Citizen Journey**: 
   - Check **Home** for new notices.
   - Use **Locker** for secure document retrieval.
   - Visit **Farmer Advisory** for daily mandi rates.
   - File a **Complaint** via voice/photo for convenience.
3. **Reward Cycle**: 
   - Admin updates complaint statuses.
   - System calculates **Impact Scores**.
   - Monthly **Leaderboard** refreshes.
   - Winners receive **Digital Certificates**.

---

### 6. Design & Accessibility
- **Visual-First UI**: Minimalist design with premium glassmorphism effects and smooth transitions (using *Outfit* and *Playfair Display* fonts).
- **Offline Reliability**: Local caching (using **Hive**) ensures that notices and downloaded documents are accessible without an active internet connection.
